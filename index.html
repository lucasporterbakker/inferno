DRAGONTURTLE NFT AUCTION

DragonTurtles are cute and collectible PFPs, each with unique attributes and colours. These legendary creatures -- possessing the longevity of the tortoise (the data) and the strength of the dragon (protocol) -- evoke several of Arweave’s core values: permanence, determination, and courage. Attributes will include references to ArDrive, Inferno, and Arweave. Each DragonTurtle has a pet PermaPunk, a pixel-art elephant.

PERMAPUNK NFT AIRDROP

In addition to the DragonTurtle NFT, each auction winner will be airdropped a PermaPunk NFT (ERC-721) minted on the Fantom blockchain, and stored on Arweave. The PermaPunk collection will be verified on NFTKEY, OperaHouse, and PaintSwap marketplaces for flippers or HODLers. The nine airdropped PermaPunks will become rare items within a limited series of 88.
